We are a group of four young undergraduate female students who are currently partaking in the AI4SocialGood Lab at McGill University. All of us are studying computer science and are steadily learning about AI and machine learning throughout the lab. This project is done in the context of the AI4SocialGood Hackathon 2018, where we are able to apply both our old and newfound knowledge to solve a social problem. Using data acquired from the internet, we trained an AI that will recognize Braille characters and translate them into English. We plan to further develop this technology to be able to recognize different forms of Braille and translate them into their corresponding languages as well as other languages. The main objective of this project is to bring awareness of Braille and to bridge the gap of communication between blind and non-blind people.
